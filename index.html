About PAX AUTOCRATICA

PAX AUTOCRATICA (Peace Under Autocracy) is a grand strategy and political simulation game that has captured the attention of players who enjoy deep strategic gameplay. PAX AUTOCRATICA combines the narrative depth of Crusader Kings with the macro-management systems of Stellaris or Victoria, creating a hardcore strategy experience. Set in a fictional near-future parallel universe filled with technological advancement yet marked by retro authoritarianism, PAX AUTOCRATICA places players in the role of a high-ranking Autocrat of an emerging or declining empire. The core themes of PAX AUTOCRATICA explore the cost of power, the balance between order and freedom, and geopolitical strategy in interstellar politics. PAX AUTOCRATICA features minimalist high-end UI design, complemented by cool-toned star maps and exquisite character portraits that bring the satirical world to life.

Game Setting & World

In PAX AUTOCRATICA, players navigate a dynamic star system network where power and control are everything. The game world is divided into distinct regions: The Core regions in PAX AUTOCRATICA are resource-rich areas where government decrees are enforced, but bureaucratic corruption can easily emerge. The Fringe zones in PAX AUTOCRATICA represent legal vacuums where smugglers and rebels thrive, serving as both expansion frontiers and potential threats. Trade Nodes in PAX AUTOCRATICA are critical strategic points - controlling these nodes allows players to dominate entire sectors' economic lifelines. The world of PAX AUTOCRATICA is not just about map painting, but about interpersonal politics and the delicate balance of maintaining an authoritarian regime.

Core Characters & Factions

PAX AUTOCRATICA features a complex character system where relationships matter. Players control The Autocrat, the ultimate decision-maker in PAX AUTOCRATICA, whose power is measured by Legitimacy and Authority values. The Cabinet in PAX AUTOCRATICA consists of ministers handling economics, research, and intelligence, each with Loyalty and Expertise ratings that determine their effectiveness. Dissidents in PAX AUTOCRATICA represent potential threats that can trigger civil wars, with their Influence and Radicalism levels determining the danger they pose. Warlords in PAX AUTOCRATICA are border commanders who are useful but prone to military independence. Managing these relationships is crucial to success in PAX AUTOCRATICA.

Gameplay Mechanics

PAX AUTOCRATICA combines multiple gameplay systems to create a unique experience. Domestic Simulation in PAX AUTOCRATICA allows players to adjust social structures by enacting Edicts. Secret Diplomacy in PAX AUTOCRATICA enables players to sign non-aggression pacts with other empires or disrupt enemy succession lines through assassination. Real-time Tactics in PAX AUTOCRATICA require players to assign commanders and decide tactical approaches such as "scorched earth" or "precision strikes" rather than relying on automatic combat. The Legitimacy System in PAX AUTOCRATICA is the game's most critical resource - if legitimacy reaches zero, even the strongest armies will face coups. This system makes PAX AUTOCRATICA a game where political management is as important as military might.
